# Code of Conduct — ColaboraPANC

## Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining a welcoming and respectful environment for all contributors, regardless of background, experience level, or role in the project.

## Expected Behaviour

- Use respectful, constructive language in all interactions.
- Accept technical criticism gracefully and offer it with care.
- Focus discussions on the technical and scientific merits of contributions.
- Respect different points of view and areas of expertise.
- Prioritise the long-term health of the project over individual preferences.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Personal attacks or demeaning comments directed at other contributors.
- Publishing private information (email, affiliation) without explicit consent.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or reviews.
- Any behaviour that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a professional scientific context.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces: issue tracker, pull requests, documentation, and any official communication channel associated with ColaboraPANC.

## Reporting

If you observe behaviour that violates this code of conduct, please report it privately to the project maintainers via the contact listed in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). All reports will be reviewed confidentially.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this code of conduct, or temporarily restrict participation by any contributor whose behaviour is deemed harmful to the community.
